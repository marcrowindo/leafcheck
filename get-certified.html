Get certified

We offer two simple ways for producers to get their products certified, depending on wether the product in question has already been tested by a reputable third party laboratory or not.

  • Lab certificate available

    The product you would like to certify already got certified by a third party laboratory.

  • No lab certificate available

    The product you would like to certify is not certified by a third party laboratory yet.

If your product has been tested by a third party laboratory in the past 12 months, then you can certify your product by simply follow these steps:

  1. Make sure your third party laboratory certificate shows the results to all required tests.
  2. Make sure your product achieved the required minimum results.
  3. Fill-in the form below and upload the lab certificate for the specific product you would like to certify.
  4. We will get back in touch with you to inform you of our decision on your application.

If your product has not or only partially been tested by a third party laboratory in the past 12 months, then you are in the right place. Leafcheck cooperates with certified laboratories in your country in order to offer reliable and fast certification of your products. To get a product tested, simply follow these steps:

  1. Fill-in the form below.
  2. Transfer the required amount for your lab tests to our account.
  3. Once we have received your data and payment we will send you an email containing the instructions for the laboratory.
  4. Please place the instructions with your product in a safe box and send it to the address of our partner laboratory.
  5. We will get back in touch with you to inform you of the results and your certification.

Test details

Required test criteria
Make sure your product was tested on the following criteria:
  1. Cannabiniod analysis, showing the top X cannabiniods contained in your product and their concentraction.
  2. Pesticides analysis
  3. Heavy metal analysis
  4. Mycotoxin analysis

If your product does not fulfill the above listed results, we will not be able to certify your product.

Please upload the certificate from the third party laboratory which shows that your product has achieved the minimum results on the lab tests which you have not selected above.
