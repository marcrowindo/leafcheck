About us

Leafcheck is a transparent and independent third party that addresses the quality problem in cannabis products. We certify produce that adheres to our high quality standards and, thereby, are making it easy for everyone to find and consume legal, safe, and high-quality cannabis products.

What our quality seal guarantees

Product legality

Certified products are in line with your local regulation. They can be purchased without fear of legal consequences.

Accurate Cannabinoid Levels

Certified products display correct levels of THC, CBD and other cannabiniods on their packaging.

No pesticides

Certified products contain no levels of pesticides that could be harmful to human health.

No heavy metals

Certified products contain no levels of heavy metals that could be harmful to human health.

No mycotoxins

Certified products contain no levels of mycotoxins that could be harmful to human health.

Entourage effect

Certified products contain a wide range of cannnabinoids to ensure a high probablility of entourage effects.

How we conduct business

Transparent revenue system

Leafcheck has a "No-Kickback-Policy": Products that are certified by Leafcheck might be listed on our website, but no earnings will be made as a result of that. Our revenue model is transparent and solely based on license fees for usage of the Leafcheck seal.

Clear certification criteria

Leafcheck clearly communicates the criteria that are required in order to get awarded the Leafcheck seal. Those criteria can be subject to change, but always under the condition of time sensitive and transparent communication.

Independent testing

Leafcheck does not conduct its own laboratory tests on cannabis products to avoid potential bias. Instead we accept certifications from high quality laboratories that are vetted by Lefcheck.
