How it works

We offer two simple ways for producers to get their products certified, depending on wether the product in question has already been tested by a reputable third party laboratory or not.

Required test criteria
Make sure your product was tested on the following criteria:
  1. Cannabiniod analysis, showing the top X cannabiniods contained in your product and their concentraction.
  2. Pesticides analysis
  3. Heavy metal analysis
  4. Mycotoxin analysis
Quality criteria
Make sure your product has achieved the following test results:
  1. THC content: <0.2%
  2. Pesticides?
  3. Heavy metals?
  4. Mycotoxins?

  • Lab certificate available

    The product you would like to certify already got certified by a third party laboratory.

  • No lab certificate available

    The product you would like to certify is not certified by a third party laboratory yet.
  • 1) Review your results

    Make sure that your certification fulfills the following criteria:

    1. Your certification must be less than 12 months old. If your certificate is 12 months old or older, we are forced to qualify your results as outdated and we kindly ask you to follow the instructions for when there is no lab certificate available.
    2. Your lab results must include all test criteria we require, as outlined here. Should your lab results not contain all criteria you will have to certify your product on the missing criteria. To do so, simply follow the instructions on our application form.

    3. The lab results which your product achieved must fulfill the quality criteria we require, as outlined here and in the application section. If your product did not achieve any or all of the required test results, then we will not be able to certify your product.
  • 2) Upload your results

    Fill in the certification application and upload your lab testsa.

  • 3) We verify your results

    After we received your certification application, we will contact the lab which certified your product in order to verify the results.

  • 4) We award the seal

    If the lab confirms your product's test results we will contact you to award your product the Leafcheck seal for 1 year.

  • 1) Send us your product

    Send in samples of the product you wish to certify our office. Please ensure that you are not just sending us the contents of the product but also its packaging becuse we will have to assess the accuracy of the packaging's claims.

  • 2) Check your results

    Make sure that the results which your product achieved match the quality criteria outlined by Leafcheck. They can be found here. Should your product not be in line with our minimum critera, then we will not be able to certify your product.

  • 3) Upload your results

    Fill in the certification application and upload your lab tests here.

  • 4) We verify your results

    After we received your certification application, we will contact the lab which certified you in order to verify your results. This will usually take no longer than 2 business days, depending on how fast the lab responds.

  • 5) We award the seal

    If the lab confirms your test results and after we have received your payment of the 1 year license fee (EUR 500.- incl. VAT) we will contact you to award your product the Leafcheck seal for 12 months.
